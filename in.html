Instruction

  1. First Click on start button.Suddenly one random color will blink.
  2. You have to click on that blinked color.
  3. After that, if you click on correct color box then again suddenly another random color will blink.
  4. Now you have to click on previously blinked color box , then click the box which is blinked now.
  5. You have to choose the colors in a sequential manner again and again in the new level.
